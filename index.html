Dominican Style

𝙻𝚘𝚜 𝚃𝚛𝚎𝚜 𝙶𝚘𝚕𝚙𝚎

This Dominican dish called "Los Tres Golpes" (three hits) is the most recognized breakfast of the Dominican Republic. It consists of fried cheese, salami and eggs. Even though it is a traditional breakfast Dominicans eat it at any time, for any meal of the day.
